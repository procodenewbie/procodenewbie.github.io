TIPS OF THE DAY

  • Bring plastic shopping bags and used batteries to supermarket/home improvement centers and recycle in designated bins.
  • Reduce one-time use plastics from the home.
  • Carry a water bottle or mason jar to fill up at water fountains instead of buying bottled water.
  • Decline receipts from merchants. Instead opt for them to be emailed to you.
  • Freeze vegetable scraps to make stock. Then compost the used scraps. Win-Win!
  • Leave a jug in the bathroom to catch running water to heat up and use for plants or toilets.
  • Use grey water to wash out recyclables.
