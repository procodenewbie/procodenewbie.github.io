WHY RECYCLE?

In our throwaway society, Americans produce about 254 million tons of trash each year according to a 2013 study conducted by the EPA. Only about 87 million tons of this waste, 34.7%, is recycled. What happens to the other 65.7%? It ends up in the overflowing landfills, get shipped to poorer countries, our littered streets and highways and the ocean.

It affects our health, quality of life and our food supply in indirect ways.
